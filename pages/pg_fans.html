Fans

        Football is global yet the culture of the game is diverse. In many countries, football is 
        ingrained into the national culture. The wearing of football kits has been a significant
        part of football fan culture since the 1973-74 season, when Leeds United's replica kit, 
        made by Admiral, was sold to football fans for the first time.

        Football stadiums exemplary the role of fans in football. The asymmetric bowl-shaped venue
        generally has the capacity to seat least 10,000 fans. All football fans revel at the thought
        of their first in-person game at their favorite team's home stadium.
