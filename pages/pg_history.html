History

        According to wikipedia, football doesn't have a classical history but its origins date
        back to eight century England. The formal rule of the games were established in the mid-19th
        century. The gameplay standard of modern day football are called the Laws of the Game. They
        were drafted by the Footall Association(FA), an organisation founded 1863
        
        As an omage to the organisation,the world's oldest football competition is the FA Cup.
        However, FIFA is the international football body. It was formed in Paris in 1904 and it adheres
        to the FA's Laws of the Game. To oversee international competition among its 211 member
        association,FIFA is subdivided into six regional confederations into which the world is divided:
        CAF (Africa), AFC (Asia and Australia), UEFA (Europe), CONCACAF (North & Central America
        and the Caribbean), OFC (Oceania) and CONMEBOL (South America).
